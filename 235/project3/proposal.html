Proposal for Project 3

Name: Ribbit!

High Concept

I wanted this game to be about a frog trying to make it back to the pond. The frog will traverse a bunch of landscapes in order to achieve said goal.

Genre

This game will for sure be a platformer. I don't think I'll have enough time to dive more into it and make it a metroidvania or something.

Platform

Preferably, anybody on Desktop web will be able to enjoy the game. I think I'd need to figure out how to handle mobile controls since by definition they don't have a keyboard.

Reason for Development

The story doesn't mean much, unless you want to make it mean something (I won't stop you).

As for the technical aspect, I have a tiny bit of experience building out subsystems for games (I built backend drawing and update calls for MonoGame and Godot projects of mine), so I don't think setting it up will prove too much of a problem. I want to make this just to test my skills, especially with a platform and language I'm not used to or 100% comfortable with.

Aesthetics

I want the game to be consisting of pixel art since I think detailed assets would take away from the story a little bit.

Gameplay

I want the player to have to aim with their mouse and press and hold space to charge up a jump.
